About Me

|
⚬
☆

Hello! My name is kristy and I am 15 years old. I am a person that has been drawing since I could remember, I had originally done traditional art but then continued into the digital world. I am not a profesional but i do enjoy drawing as it gives me something to do.

My art style has come a long way since. I first started off with a very chibi style, big head and super small body. As soon I saw other people drawing super complex styles I decided to change my style to what is now shown. I have changed my shading tecnique a lot in the past but decided that I'm happy with where I am at the moment.

Tools:

  • Ibis-Paint
  • Ipad + Stylus
